Submit a Story

Pull Up a Chair is looking for stories for its upcoming show. As a storytelling variety show we accept stories in all manner of media and formats, including musical performances, visual art and multimedia. Please see the submission guidelines below.

Submission Guidelines

  1. Stories must be true. Some poetic license is allowed but the events of the story must be based on true events.
  2. All performances should average between 7 and 12 minutes in length
  3. Please include any special technical requirements or staging requests in the submission.
  4. Pull Up a Chair is a storytelling show. Reading from notes or a script is discouraged.
